QueueDrop Is a Solution!

QueueDrop helps you avoid queues that you encounter in everyday life. Once you get QueueDrop, this unique mobile application lets you search for all places that are using the “queue-number” – system. You can also look for places near you by letting the app use the location on your device. After you find the desired store or institution, you can simply take your digital queue number. Having your queue number, QueueDrop will show you your place in the line and notify you when there are four persons before you in the queue. The application will notify you again when there is only one person in front of you. If you feel that the notification is not enough as a warning, QueueDrop lets you set up an alarm that will alerts you instead of sending the notifications. App is also showing the average waiting time for your location at a given moment.

Our application is very easy to use. It has a simple design that makes all features understandable and content easily readable. It also has a voice input possibility which allows our users to manage the application by speaking to their device. Likewise, instead of reading data from the screen, QueueDrop can read all information for you. This can be done by double clicking anywhere on your screen.
